About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by [Bibek Pandeya], The Foodie Home has been serving delicious meals and creating memorable dining experiences for customers in Hasanpur-5 Street, Dhangadhi city, Nepal. What started as a small family-owned restaurant has now grown into a beloved culinary destination known for its exceptional food and warm hospitality.

Our Mission

At The Foodie Home, our mission is to delight our customers with delicious food, exceptional service, and a welcoming atmosphere. We are committed to using fresh, high-quality ingredients, supporting local suppliers, and creating a positive impact in our community.
