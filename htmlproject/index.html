About Our Restaurant


Welcome to the Foodie Resturant


Located in the heart of Hasanpur-5 Street in Dhangadhi city, Nepal, Foodie Home is a beloved culinary destination for food enthusiasts of all ages. Our restaurant is committed to serving delicious and satisfying meals that cater to a variety of tastes and preferences.

At Foodie Home, we take pride in offering a diverse menu featuring a fusion of local and international cuisines. From hearty comfort food to exotic flavors from around the world, there's something to tantalize every palate. Our dishes are prepared using fresh, high-quality ingredients and cooked to perfection by our talented chefs.
